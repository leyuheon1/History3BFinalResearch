In this page, we will go over the state of the world in the 16th century.
The major events that have occured in this period are
1) Columbus's "discovery of the New World"
2) The war between Chosun(Korea), China(Ming dynasty) and Japan
3) Habsburgs bid over European dominance
4) Ottoman expansion in the Europe
We will discuss about the Habsburgs in the next section. Here we will briefly discuss about the Columbus's discovery of the New World, the Imjin War, and the Ottoman expansion in Europe
In the Iberian peninsula, the Spain has finally driven out the last remnant of the Muslim forces from Granada in the year 1492. The Reconquista was finally accomplished. The two monarchs, Ferdinand of Aragon and Isabella of Castile, are very important because not only they united the whole Iberian peninsula but also they sponsored Columbus's voyage.source Moreover, their daughter, Joanna of Castile, married the Habsburg prince, Philip I of Habsburg. This led to the golden age of the Habsburg dynasty when the Habsburg family ruled Spain, Austria, Hungary, significant part of Italy, and Netherlands at same time. But for now, we focus on the Columbus's voyage to the Americas.
