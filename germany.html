The Napoleonic Wars left a deep scar in Europe. 2.5 million soldiers and 1 million civilians were killed. So the one thing that all European monarchs agreed upon at the Congress of Vienna was to restore stability in Europe. Restoring and maintaining the balance of Europe was the most important agenda fro European leaders. That is why France was saved from harsh treatment as European monarchs did not want to risk further destabilizing France which could result in another revolution. So the adventurist diplomacy which might result in another war was frowned upon and most nations strived to maintain the status quo. source1 source2
